Welcome! In this website a student can easily send their information and data to BISE Pakistan (Board Of Intermidiate And Secondary Education Pakistan) and can get his/her Roll-No. Slip within few days. Here you have to enter your data that is asked from you by us. Even you belong to any country you can easily fill this registration form and can get your slip and can easily take part in examination. Here you have to enter simple information like names, addresses, phone numbers, state information and Gmail .etc. After filling this form you can get the link of your Slip in Gmail, message or other apps. If there was any problem BOARD will contact you personally.
